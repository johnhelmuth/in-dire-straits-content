## Content session refactoring


Since I hope to be starting the second campaign in this setting soon, I
refactored the configuration and some code so that the session summaries are
grouped by campaign.

This included separating out the news stories and campaign session summaries
from the "tatterpedia" and "rules and systems" sections, mostly to simplify the
way the breadcrumbs work.
