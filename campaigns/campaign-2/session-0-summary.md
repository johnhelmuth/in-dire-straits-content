We got a start on setting up the specifics of the tone and PC activities during
session 0 with a mix of new and old players. We included an overview of the
cluster in which the players will be acting, how it is connected to the rest of
known space, (not yet), what the PCs will be doing that has drawn them together,
and what types of problems they will run into.

They will be the crew of a tugboat working for the Port Authority of the
New Earth Directorate (NED), based at the space station *Port Rail* orbiting the
planet Gaia above the capitol city of the New Earth Directory.

The NED recently won a war of secession, and the soldiers have been coming home
and changing the tenor of society.
