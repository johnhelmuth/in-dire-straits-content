Trio, based on discussions about the Phase Trio on

the space station above the capital city of the
planet [Gaia](/tatterpedia/places/clusters/isolated-cluster/gaia)
in the [Torrin system](/tatterpedia/places/clusters/isolated-cluster/torrin).

## Episode 4

[Scout](/characters/scout-4e0061377), [Dwabit](/characters/dwabit), and
[Deli](/characters/delilah) needed to impound the small _Salaran_ merchant ship
_Lullaby_ from a parking orbit; _Lullaby has stayed there too long with no
communication response to traffic control.

The shipowner is Wolk Jammer, whose shuttle is docked at the station hub. He
and his engineer Blair Coiny took the space elevator to the capital city 3 weeks
prior.

The crew had to hook up their tug to the _Lullaby_ and take it out to the
impound yard on _Castillo_ where it will not be a hazard to traffic.

[Captain Martinez](/characters/captain-martinez) told the crew he had meetings
all day and the crew could handle the job on their own.

The standard process in these situations is to try to hail the ship in case
anyone is still there, and if no one responds, put the hook and move it.

Before heading out Deli tried to reach Captain Jammer on the planetary comms
system, and gets no response. She leaves a message regarding the steps needed to
reclaim the ship and info regarding ferry services.

The crew set out on the _NED Tugboat_. As they approached the _Lullaby's_
parking orbit, they noticed a pipe-scooter floating nearby.

Deli hailed the _Lullaby_ while Dwabit maneuvered the *Tugboat* as close to the
_Lullaby_ as he could, and Scout got into his EVA gear in preparation to hook up
the _Lullaby_ to the tug.

Deli's hail was answered by a person who was obviously not used to
communicating over the radio. The person identified herself as Shelley Bowers.
She appeared to be a young woman, possibly underage, and was scared because no
one had come out to the ship in the two weeks since she got there.

Dwabit smoothly maneuvered the _Tugboat_ to within kissing distance of
_Lullaby_.

Scout did what Scout does and maneuvered over to the pipe-scooter to check it
out before heading to the _Lullaby_. He discovered it appeared to be tricked out
with pink stickers and the colors of the teen "gang" known as the _Blackadders_.
He continued on his path around the _Lullaby_ to make sure nothing else was
weird.

Deli used the Port Authority override to get into the _Lullaby's_ system so she
would be able to use that ship's systems to track Shelley and to talk directly
to her. She convinced Shelley to get her suit and helmet on, and to go
to the airlock so that they could get her off the ship and over to the
_Tugboat_.

The _Tugboat_'s proximity sensors notified Dwabit that another ship was
maneuvering towards them. The ship had its transponder turned off, an illegal act. 
Deli notified PA Dispatch about the lack of transponder and then tried 
contacting the new ship.

Dwabit did a focused scan of the new ship and noticed it was powering up its
beam weapon system.

Scout aborted his careful survey of the _Lullaby_, and quickly went directly
over the top and into the open airlock facing the _Tugboat_. He started
pressurizing the airlock to get Shelley out, who was standing on the other side
of the inner airlock door.

Assuming that the new ship was targeting the _Tugboat_, Dwabit quickly 
maneuvered behind the _Lullaby_. Shelley used her control of the _Lullaby_ to 
blow its airlock evacuating all the air from the ship, throwing Shelley into 
Scout, who grabbed onto her as they were both quickly forced away from the ship.

The new ship fired their beam weapon targeting the _Lullaby_, which ruptured its
hull and caused a failure of, the reactor containment system. The _Lullaby_ blew 
completely apart; the _Tugboat_, Scout, and Shelley avoided most of the blast. 
The new ship turned and escaped into the traffic around _Port Rails_.

Dwabit circled the _Tugboat_ around and picked up the pipe-scooter, Scout, and
Shelley, and they returned to base.

When they docked in the PRPA hangar bay, Captain Martinez was there to bring
Shelley to her parents, waiting in the PRPA Chief Administrator's office. Deli
noticed that his usual bluster was disturbing Shelley's composure, she forced 
him to allow her to join them in the office. Martinez capitulated, but will now
try to diminish Deli's role in the organization, and possibly get her fired.
