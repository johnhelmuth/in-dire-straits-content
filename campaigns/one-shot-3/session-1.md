While traveling back to Alodon on Golden Seed from Abilene, the spacer merchant
Dalton Murrieta ran across Ikari Kintsugi at the inn on the road to Abilene.
Kintsugi was an Alodon pick-pocket he had worked with a few years earlier. Back
then, she had picked the pocket of another spacer merchant and didn't know how
to fence the off-world data-chip she had lifted.

"Murri" had been quick to promise her to turn it into one of those fancy spacer
pocket-watches and some cash once he got off-world, he would be back soon. That
was 3 years ago. He decided when he spotted her serving drinks to avoid any 
unpleasantness with her by staying at the back of the common room and trying to 
remain unobtrusive while he ate his own meal.

When Lady Zanetti (who he owed money to) walked through the door with her 
bodyguard Gus Matronas and ordered a meal and some ale, he decided that he
would continue to follow that same strategy.


This particular evening, Kintsugi had left Alodon due to some heat surrounding
the acquisition of a piece of jewelry from Lady Zanetti. She was working at the 
inn when Lady Zanetti and her bodyguard walked in. Fortunately, she had covered 
her golden scars with makeup, and the chance of them recognizing her was slight. 
She decided to tough it out and hope that she wasn't recognized.

Later, she got a good look at the guy who had been lurking at the back of the 
common room, and recognized Murri. She had wondered for 3 years if she got taken 
on the deal for the data chip, but here he was. Before she could find out if he
had her fancy pocket-watch and her money, they heard a scream from the top of
stairs.
