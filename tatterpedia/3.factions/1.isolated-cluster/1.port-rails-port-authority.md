title: Port Rails Port Authority
---

* The Port Authority offices are on Floor 25, sector 17, with bay doors that
  face out (away from Gaia.)
* The PRPA is responsible for the security and safety of the traffic coming and
  going through Port Rails down to the planet. This includes space traffic
  control, maintaining safe space traffic routes, docking assignments and
  assistance, managing docking fees, and customs inspections of docked ships and
  passengers.
* The PRPA is led by the PRPA Executive, an appointed position currently held
  by _Khira_.
